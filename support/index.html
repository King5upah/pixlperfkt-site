Frequently asked

Where are my projects stored?
On your device. If you enable iCloud, they also sync to your personal iCloud Drive so they're available across your iPhone, iPad, and Mac. The developer never has access to them.

Does it work offline?
Yes. PixlPerfkt is fully offline — it makes no network requests and includes no analytics, tracking, or ads.

How do I export my art?
Open a project and tap Export. You can export PNG at multiple scales, or export every size variant at once as a single ZIP, then share it anywhere via the system share sheet.

How do I use AR mode?
On iPhone and iPad, tap the AR (ARKit) button in the canvas toolbar, grant camera access, then aim at a flat surface to place your canvas and paint it in augmented reality. AR requires a physical device and isn't available on Mac.

Is it one purchase for all platforms?
Yes. A single purchase unlocks PixlPerfkt on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
